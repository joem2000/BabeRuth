Joe Molanare





About Us




Joe has coached for 2 years with the New Hartford Babe Ruth team.
Joe graduated from New Hartford in 2019, and is currently a Senior at SUNY Poly.
He played baseball at New Hartford for 6 years and at SUNY Poly for 2 years.

This past 2022 Summer was his first season as Head coach
and was able to help bring the team to the championship game.
Joe's goal is not only to win, but to have fun!
He believes kids should stop thinking so much and to just play baseball!

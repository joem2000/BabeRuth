About Us










We expect our players to come to every practice and game. Must be notified otherwise. We are a competitive team.
Our players need to earn their spot on the field. Everyone must hustle, show respect to coaches and teammates,
good attitude, and to work hard. If players do not show up to practice without a valid excuse, they will not play the next game.

THERE IS NO BULLYING! THIS COULD RESULT IN BEING REMOVED OFF THE ROSTER!

These are very simple expectations, following these will help us win ball games and will have a fun season!
